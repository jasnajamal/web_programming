Festivals

Festivals are community celebrations often marking religious or cultural events.

Religious

  • Diwali
  • Christmas
  • Eid al-Fitr

National

  1. Independence Day
  2. Republic Day
  3. Gandhi Jayanti

Seasonal

Holi:
This is the "festival of colors" that marks the end of winter and the arrival of spring.
Onam:
Kerala's harvest festival, celebrated with boat races, traditional dances, and Onasadya.
